Welcome to Mitho Mitho Mitho

Nestled in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal, Mitho Mitho Mitho is more than a hotel — it's a celebration of Nepali flavour. From the first sip of our freshly brewed tea to the last bite of our signature ramen, every moment here is crafted to make you feel at home.

We serve authentic Nepali cuisine, from dal bhat to momos, thukpa to chiya, and our world-famous Nepali Ramen Noodles. Whether you're dining in or taking home a raw kit to recreate the magic yourself — this is the best choice you'll make today.

Meet the Founder

Shreya Bista

🎓 Culinary Arts Graduate 💼 Entrepreneur 🇳🇵 Proud Nepali

Shreya Bista 💗 a passionate and creative entrepreneur who truly loves Nepali culture and food. Her dream is simple — to give every visitor a warm, memorable taste of Nepal. She carefully works on recipes, guides the team, and makes sure every guest feels welcome. For her, *food is a way to show love.* a place where people can enjoy good food, feel happy, and experience true Nepali hospitality. Shreya founded this hotel with one dream: to give every visitor an unforgettable taste of Nepal.

Smart, dedicated, and deeply rooted in her heritage, Shreya hand-curates every recipe, trains every chef, and personally ensures the warmest hospitality. Her philosophy? "Food is love made visible."
